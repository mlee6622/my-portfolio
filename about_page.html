ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Madeline Lee, a psychology major with a minor in user experience design. I've always been interested in understanding how people think, feel, and act. Since discovering user experience, I've focused this interest in observing how people interact with digital experiences to create human-centered designs. Most of my experiences lie in conducting user research and establishing the operations behind it as I continue to refine my creative abilities. Outside of school and design, I'm a big fan of crocheting, baking treats, and experimenting with small creative projects that spark joy.

This portfolio showcases my academic projects, UX design work, and experiences that reflect my curiosity for both research and creativity.
